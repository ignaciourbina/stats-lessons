Understanding Conditional Probability

How known information changes the likelihood of events

Brief Introduction

Conditional probability helps us determine how the probability of an event changes when we have additional information. This concept is essential in real-world decision-making, from weather forecasting to medical testing. This lesson builds your understanding through simple examples, visual tools, and the formal definition of conditional probability.
