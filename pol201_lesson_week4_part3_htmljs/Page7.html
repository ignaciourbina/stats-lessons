Please consider the following question.

A researcher claims that a new drug is effective in curing a particular disease. Historically, the cure rate for the disease without any treatment is 30%. A clinical trial with 150 patients who received the new drug showed that 55 of them were cured. A hypothesis test is conducted at the 95% confidence level and is a two-tailed test.

1. What is the null hypothesis (H0) and the alternative hypothesis (H1) for this test?

2. What is the critical value and the test statistic (Z) for this test?

3. Based on the comparison of the test statistic and the critical value, what conclusion should be drawn about the effectiveness of the new drug?
